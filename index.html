Standard Curriculum

kinder (3-6) years

Our Junior School imparts a well designed and highly thought provoking curriculum for the learners aged 3+ to 6 years (Playgroup to KG), which is relevant to the modern world and challenging as well.

The O’ and A’ Level programmes of our Middle School provide a highly demanding, competitive and expansive educational qualifications. Bengali and Bangladesh Studies are compulsory in O’ Level.
